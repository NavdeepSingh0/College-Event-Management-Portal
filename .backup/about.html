About CU Events Platform

Connecting the Chandigarh University community through memorable experiences

Our Mission

To create a seamless platform that brings together students, faculty, and organizations for enriching campus experiences at Chandigarh University.

The CU Events platform was built to solve a simple problem: making it easy for 30,000+ students to discover, register for, and participate in campus events. From tech hackathons to cultural fests, career workshops to sports tournaments — every event at CU deserves to be discovered.

🏫 About Chandigarh University

Chandigarh University (CU), located in Gharuan, Mohali, Punjab, is one of India's premier educational institutions. With 30,000+ students across Engineering, Business, Design, Sciences, Law, and Hospitality, CU is a vibrant hub of innovation and learning.

Our campus boasts state-of-the-art facilities, 150+ student-led clubs and societies, and a culture that encourages holistic development beyond the classroom.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for events? +

Simply browse events, click "Register Now" on any event page. You'll need to login with your CU ID first.

Can I create events as a student? +

Yes! Any registered student can create events through the "Create Event" page. Fill out the multi-step form and publish.

How do I get event certificates? +

Certificates are automatically generated for events that offer them. You can download them from your dashboard after attending.

What if an event is cancelled? +

You'll receive a notification and email if an event you registered for is cancelled. Any paid fees will be refunded.

How do I report an issue? +

Use the contact form above or email us at events@cumail.in. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Can I get a refund for paid events? +

Refunds are available up to 24 hours before the event start time. Contact the event organizer or our support team.
